## Notes & Observations

1. **SEO is Long-Game:** Full results (page 1 rankings) take 3-6 months. Be patient.

2. **Content Quality Over Quantity:** One great 3,000-word article beats 3 mediocre 1,000-word articles.

3. **Consistency is Key:** Post on same schedule, maintain quality, engage with audience regularly.

4. **Data-Driven:** Everything must be measured. Use A/B testing, analytics, and iterate.

5. **Audience First:** Create content that solves their problems, not what we want to sell.

6. **Partnerships:** Early partnerships with VR companies, educational institutions, and training centers can accelerate growth.

7. **Community:** Build a community around VR training. Forums, webinars, networking events.

8. **Budget Allocation:** Recommend 60% content, 20% paid ads, 20% tools/infrastructure.
